4.3 ARM entity definitions

This subclause specifies the ARM entities for this module. Each ARM application entity is an atomic element that embodies a unique application concept and contains attributes specifying the data elements of the entity. The ARM entities and definitions are specified below.

4.3.1 Apex   

An Apex is a type of Derived_non_feature_shape_element that is either a point that is derived by projecting a conical surface or the common point that is derived by projecting two or more conical surfaces. The Shape_element from which the Apex is derived are associated with the Apex by the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that are specified as the deriving_relationships.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Apex
  SUBTYPE OF (Derived_non_feature_shape_element);
END_ENTITY;
(*

Informal propositions:

IP1: The common intersection of three or more planes or two curves shall be treated as a Geometric_intersection rather than as an Apex.

IP2: The form of the Shape_elements from which the Apex is derived shall be consistent with conical surfaces from which an Apex can be derived.

4.3.2 Boundary_size_characteristic   

A Boundary_size_characteristic is either a Diametrical_boundary_size_characteristic or an Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic. A Boundary_size_characteristic is a definition that characterizes the size of the Boundary_size_characteristic.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Boundary_size_characteristic
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (Diametrical_boundary_size_characteristic,
                                Opposing_boundary_set_size_characteristic));
  tolerance_value : Length_data_element;
  maximum_tolerance_value : OPTIONAL Length_data_element;
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

tolerance_value: specifies the Length_data_element that represents the tolerance size for the Boundary_size_characteristic.

maximum_tolerance_value: specifies the Length_data_element that represents the maximum size of the Boundary_size_characteristic. The value of this attribute need not be specified.

4.3.3 Centre_axis   

A Centre_axis is a type of Centre_of_symmetry that is axial in form. The Shape_element from which the Centre_axis is derived are associated with the Centre_axis by the Shape_element_deriving_relationship that are specified as the deriving_relationships.

NOTE 1   The attribute deriving_relationships is declared in the Derived_shape_element entity of which this entity is a subtype.

NOTE 2   The precise interpretation of a Centre_axis is context dependent. That is, the distinction is not made between an axis of a feature and a derived median line of a feature as defined in 1.3 of ASME Y14.5M.

EXAMPLE    A Position_tolerance and a Straightness_tolerance may be applied to the same Centre_axis of a cylindrical Shape_element.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Centre_axis
  SUBTYPE OF (Centre_of_symmetry);
WHERE
  WR1: NOT EXISTS(SELF\Shape_element.description);
END_ENTITY;
(*

Formal propositions:

WR1: The description shall not be populated.

Informal propositions:

IP1: The form of the Shape_elements from which the Centre_axis is derived shall be consistent with the form of shape elements from which a centre axis can be derived.

4.3.4 Centre_of_symmetry   

A Centre_of_symmetry is a type of Derived_non_feature_shape_element that is derived by determining the geometric centre of one or more Shape_element. A Centre_of_symmetry is one of a Centre_axis, a Centre_plane, or a Centre_point.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Centre_of_symmetry
  ABSTRACT SUPERTYPE OF (ONEOF (Centre_axis,
                                Centre_plane,
                                Centre_point))
  SUBTYPE OF (Derived_non_feature_shape_element);
END_ENTITY;
(*

4.3.5 Centre_plane

4.3.9 Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary   

A Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is a type of Tolerance_zone_boundary that is conical in form. The shape of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is defined by a frustum the size of which is specified at the surfaces that define the ends of the frustum.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary
  SUBTYPE OF (Tolerance_zone_boundary);
INVERSE
  boundary_and_surface_relationships : SET[2:2] OF Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship FOR sized_tolerance_zone_boundary;
WHERE
  WR1: NOT EXISTS(SELF\Shape_element.description);
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

boundary_and_surface_relationships: specifies an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary is dependent on the existence of two Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship that specify the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary as their sized_tolerance_zone_boundary. There shall be exactly two Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship for a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary.

Formal propositions:

WR1: The description shall not be populated.

4.3.10 Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship   

A Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship is a relationship that associates a Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary with one of the two physical_feature_or_part_template at which the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary diameter is specified.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship;
  sized_tolerance_zone_boundary : Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary;
  terminating_surface : physical_feature_or_part_template;
  terminating_surface_datum : OPTIONAL Datum_defined_by_feature;
INVERSE
  size_characteristic : Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic FOR sized_boundary_at_surface;
WHERE
  WR1: NOT EXISTS(terminating_surface_datum) OR (terminating_surface :=: terminating_surface_datum\Datum_defined_by_feature.defined_by);
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

sized_tolerance_zone_boundary: specifies the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary the diameter of which is specified at the physical_feature_or_part_template specified as the terminating_surface.

terminating_surface: specifies the physical_feature_or_part_template at which the diameter is specified for the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary.

terminating_surface_datum: specifies a role of the Datum_defined_by_feature for the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship. The value of this attribute need not be specified.

size_characteristic: specifies an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship is dependent on the existence of the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_diametrical_size_characteristic that specifies the Conical_tolerance_zone_boundary_and_surface_relationship as its sized_boundary_at_surface.

Formal propositions:

WR1: If there is a terminating_surface_datum then the terminating_surface shall be the same surface that defines the terminating_surface_datum.

4.3.16 Datum_based_direction_element   

A Datum_based_direction_element is a definition of a shape element that has the form of a half-plane and is used in conjunction with the definition of another half-plane shape element to define a Datum_based_vector_orientation. In this instance a half-plane has the form of a plane except it is bounded on one side by a straight line. The half-plane shape element that the Datum_based_direction_element defines is oriented with respect to three Datum_plane.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Datum_based_direction_element;
  direction : Datum_based_direction_vector;
  angle_value : Angle_data_element;
  base_datum_plane : Datum_plane;
  direction_of_half_datum_plane : Datum_based_direction_vector;
INVERSE
  elements_of : SET[1:?] OF Datum_based_vector_orientation FOR direction_components;
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

direction: specifies the Datum_based_direction_vector that defines the orientation of the angle between the half-plane shape element defined by the Datum_based_direction_element and half of the Datum_plane specified by the base_datum_plane. The angle is measured from half of the base Datum_plane to the defined half-plane shape element in a clockwise direction when viewed in the direction of the Datum_based_direction_vector.

angle_value: specifies the Angle_data_element that represents the magnitude of the angle between the half-plane shape element defined by the Datum_based_direction_element and half of the Datum_plane specified as the base_datum_plane.

base_datum_plane: specifies the Datum_plane from which the half-plane shape element defined by the Datum_based_direction_element is oriented.

direction_of_half_datum_plane: specifies the Datum_based_direction_vector of a Datum_plane that intersects the base Datum_plane and indicates the half of the base Datum_plane from which the half-plane shape element defined by the Datum_based_direction_element is oriented.

elements_of: specifies an inverse relationship that specifies that the existence of the Datum_based_direction_element is dependent on the existence of one or more Datum_based_vector_orientation that specifies the Datum_based_direction_element as one of their direction_components.

positive_direction_vector: specifies a boolean value that indicates whether the Datum_based_direction_vector is in a positive or negative sense. A value of TRUE indicates that the Datum_based_direction_vector is in the positive sense. A value of FALSE indicates that the Datum_based_direction_vector is in the negative sense.

4.3.18 Datum_based_length_measure   

A Datum_based_length_measure is a dimensional measure of length that is taken from a defined Datum.

EXAMPLE    Figure 1 illustrates the usage of a Datum_based_length_measure.




Figure 1 —  Datum_based_length_measure

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Datum_based_length_measure;
  measure : Length_data_element;
  measure_orientation : descriptive_or_geometric_orientation;
  of_datum : Datum_plane;
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

measure: specifies the role of the Length_data_element for the Datum_based_length_measure.

measure_orientation: specifies the role of the Direction for the Datum_based_length_measure or specifies the role of the string 'normal' or specifies the role of the string 'reversed'. 'Normal' shall be interpreted as a description equivalent to the three dimensional concept of a vector parallel to the normal of the plane that is the datum upon which the Datum_based_length_measure is based. 4.3.80 Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification   

A Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification is a type of Tolerance_zone_definition in which the extent of the Tolerance_zone is defined. A Tolerance_zone_definition_with_per_unit_size_specification is used to define a Tolerance_zone when the design requirements are such that abrupt variations in the toleranced Shape_element must be limited. In essence, the thickness, or the diameter, of the Tolerance_zone is defined by the Boundary_size_characteristic and the extent is defined by the Tolerance_zone_per_unit_size_specification. Each element of the toleranced Shape_element that has the same extent as the Tolerance_zone must lie within the Tolerance_zone when the Tolerance_zone is positioned coincident with the element.
