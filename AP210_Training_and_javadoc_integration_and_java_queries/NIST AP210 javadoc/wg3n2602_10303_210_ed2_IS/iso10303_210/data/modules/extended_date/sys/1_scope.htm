TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION ISO/TS 10303-1776:2008(E)

Industrial automation systems and integration —
Product data representation and exchange —
Part 1776:
Application module: Extended date

1 Scope

This part of ISO 10303 specifies the application module Extended date. The following are within the scope of this part of ISO 10303:

  • definition of a year, year and month, year and ordinal date, year and week and day of week;
  • items within the scope of application module Date time, ISO/TS 10303-1010.
