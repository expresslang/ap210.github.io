4.2 ARM entity definitions

This subclause specifies the ARM entities for this module. Each ARM application entity is an atomic element that embodies a unique application concept and contains attributes specifying the data elements of the entity. The ARM entities and definitions are specified below.

4.2.1 Connector_based_interconnect_definition   

A Connector_based_interconnect_definition is the linkage between two physical connectors that specifies the existence of a mated state of that pair of connectors in the context of an assembly that includes the components those connectors are related to. The connectors each are part of an item in the product for which the Physical_unit_interconnect_definition referenced by associated_definition is a definition.

NOTE    A Connector_based_interconnect_definition can be considered to be a minimal or high level depiction of the linkage between the items included in the product.

EXAMPLE    Consider a case where a computer supplier provides a detached cable between a monitor and a computer as part of a kit. The kit includes at least the following three items: a cable, a monitor and a computer. A Connector_based_interconnect_definition for connecting the computer to the monitor cable would indicate a linkage between the 'video out' connector on the computer and the 'computer' connector on the monitor cable.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Connector_based_interconnect_definition;
  name : STRING;
  associated_definition : Physical_unit_interconnect_definition;
  connector_in_uv_1 : Next_assembly_usage;
  connector_in_uv_2 : Next_assembly_usage;
  component_1 : Next_assembly_usage;
  component_2 : Next_assembly_usage;
UNIQUE
  UR1: name, associated_definition;
WHERE
  WR1: 'ASSEMBLY_MODULE_WITH_PACKAGED_CONNECTOR_COMPONENT_ARM.PACKAGED_CONNECTOR_COMPONENT' IN TYPEOF(connector_in_uv_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view);
  WR2: 'ASSEMBLY_MODULE_WITH_PACKAGED_CONNECTOR_COMPONENT_ARM.PACKAGED_CONNECTOR_COMPONENT' IN TYPEOF(connector_in_uv_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view);
  WR3: 'PHYSICAL_UNIT_USAGE_VIEW_ARM.PART_USAGE_VIEW' IN TYPEOF(connector_in_uv_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view);
  WR4: 'PHYSICAL_UNIT_USAGE_VIEW_ARM.PART_USAGE_VIEW' IN TYPEOF(connector_in_uv_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view);
  WR5: EXISTS(connector_in_uv_1\Assembly_component_relationship.location_indicator);
  WR6: EXISTS(connector_in_uv_2\Assembly_component_relationship.location_indicator);
  WR7: EXISTS(component_1\Assembly_component_relationship.location_indicator);
  WR8: EXISTS(component_2\Assembly_component_relationship.location_indicator);
  WR9: component_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view\Definition_based_product_occurrence.derived_from = connector_in_uv_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view;
  WR10: component_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view\Definition_based_product_occurrence.derived_from = connector_in_uv_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view;
  WR11: component_1 <> component_2;
  WR12: component_1 <> connector_in_uv_1;
  WR13: connector_in_uv_1 <> component_2;
  WR14: component_1 <> connector_in_uv_2;
  WR15: connector_in_uv_2 <> component_2;
  WR16: 'PHYSICAL_UNIT_INTERCONNECT_DEFINITION_ARM.DEFINITION_BASED_PART_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(component_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view);
  WR17: 'PHYSICAL_UNIT_INTERCONNECT_DEFINITION_ARM.DEFINITION_BASED_PART_OCCURRENCE' IN TYPEOF(component_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.related_view);
  WR18: associated_definition = component_1\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view;
  WR19: associated_definition = component_2\Product_occurrence_definition_relationship.relating_view;
END_ENTITY;
(*

Attribute definitions:

name: the words by which the Connector_based_interconnect_definition is known.

associated_definition: specifies the role of the Physical_unit_interconnect_definition for the Connector_based_interconnect_definition.

connector_in_uv_1: specifies one role of a Next_assembly_usage for the Connector_based_interconnect_definition.

connector_in_uv_2: specifies one role of a Next_assembly_usage for the Connector_based_interconnect_definition.

component_1: specifies one role of a Next_assembly_usage for the Connector_based_interconnect_definition.

component_2: specifies one role of a Next_assembly_usage for the Connector_based_interconnect_definition.

Formal propositions:

UR1: The name shall be unique within the context of the associated_definition.

WR1: Only a Packaged_connector_component shall be referenced by the connector_in_uv_1 as related_view.

WR2: Only a Packaged_connector_component shall be referenced by the connector_in_uv_2 as related_view.

WR3: Only a Part_usage_view shall be referenced by the connector_in_uv_1 as relating_view.

WR4: Only a Part_usage_view shall be referenced by the connector_in_uv_2 as relating_view.

WR5: The location_indicator shall exist for connector_in_uv_1.

WR6: The location_indicator shall exist for connector_in_uv_2.

WR7: The location_indicator shall exist for component_1.

WR8: The location_indicator shall exist for component_2.

WR9: The derived_from of related_view of component_1 shall be relating_view for connector_in_uv_1.

WR10: The derived_from of related_view of component_2 shall be relating_view for connector_in_uv_2.

WR11: component_1 shall not be component_2.

WR12: component_1 shall not be connector_in_uv_1.

WR13: connector_in_uv_1 shall not be component_2.

WR14: component_1 shall not be connector_in_uv_2.

WR15: connector_in_uv_2 shall not be component_2.

WR16: The related_view of component_1 shall be the Definition_based_part_occurrence.

WR17: The related_view of component_2 shall be the Definition_based_part_occurrence.

WR18: The relating_view of component_1 shall be the associated_definition.

WR19: The relating_view of component_2 shall be the associated_definition.

4.2.2 Physical_unit_interconnect_definition   

A Physical_unit_interconnect_definition is a type of Part_view_definition A Physical_unit_interconnect_definition is referenced by members of Connector_based_interconnect_definition that correspond to the mated connectors for the sub assemblies included in the design. A Physical_unit_interconnect_definition does not include the detailed wiring definition of the cables and assemblies included. A Physical_unit_interconnect_definition does not include the mating conditions or geometric information needed to describe the position and orientation of the items in the Physical_unit_interconnect_definition.

EXPRESS specification:

*)
ENTITY Physical_unit_interconnect_definition
  SUBTYPE OF (Part_view_definition);
WHERE
  WR1: SIZEOF(QUERY(nauo <* USEDIN(SELF, 'ASSEMBLY_STRUCTURE_ARM.PRODUCT_OCCURRENCE_DEFINITION_RELATIONSHIP.RELATING_VIEW') | (('PHYSICAL_UNIT_DESIGN_VIEW_ARM.NEXT_ASSEMBLY_USAGE_OCCURRENCE_RELATIONSHIP' IN TYPEOF(nauo)) XOR ('ASSEMBLY_STRUCTURE_ARM.NEXT_ASSEMBLY_USAGE' IN TYPEOF(nauo))) )) = 0;
END_ENTITY;
(*

Formal propositions:

WR1: Each Product_occurrence_definition_relationship that references the Physical_unit_interconnect_definition as a relating_view shall be a complex of both a Next_assembly_usage and a Next_assembly_usage_occurrence_relationship or the Product_occurrence_definition_relationship shall not be either a Next_assembly_usage or a Next_assembly_usage_occurrence_relationship.



*)
END_SCHEMA;  -- Physical_unit_interconnect_definition_arm
